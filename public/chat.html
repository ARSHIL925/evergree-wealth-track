What is a SIP?

A Systematic Investment Plan is a way to invest a fixed rupee amount into a mutual fund every month. It builds discipline and uses rupee-cost averaging — you buy more units when prices fall, fewer when they rise — smoothing volatility over the long run.

How does compound interest work?

Compounding earns interest on both your principal and the interest already earned. A ₹5,000 monthly SIP at 12% for 20 years grows to about ₹50 lakhs even though you only contributed ₹12 lakhs.

How safe is my data on Evergreen?

Every table uses Row-Level Security scoped to your user id. Traffic is HTTPS-only with strict security headers. See the Security page inside the app for the full list of controls.

Is UPI real money?

UPI links generated in your Profile open your bank app to send money to the UPI id you saved. Subscriptions on this site are demo-mode: paying awards in-app tokens, no real charge.
